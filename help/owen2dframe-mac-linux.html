  B → L
  ctrl+B → M (or shift+B → M)
  alt+B → R

  L → L

  R → R




One by WACOM tablet (mod. no. CTL-472).
Gaomo Graphics IPS Pen Display (mod. no. PDP1161).
map stylus/pen w/ 2 buttons (a,b) + tip (t) pressed on tablet:
  standard tablet behavior:
    t → L
    pen hovering over tablet no more than 0.5cm away and pressing a → M
    pen hovering over tablet no more than 0.5cm away and pressing b → R
  additionally (in interactive 2d only):
    ctrl+t (or shift+t) → M
    alt+t → R
  where a=button closer to tip, b=button further from tip, and t=tip pressed on tablet
