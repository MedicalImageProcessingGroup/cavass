Advanced filtering for noise suppression

There are more advanced filtering operation for the noise suppression:

Scale Based Diffusion Filtering and Scale Based Averaging
    Step-by-step
  Scale Based Diffusion Filtering

In order to attain this purpose the following steps need to be followed: Click on Preprocess –> Scene Operations -> Filter

 

On Down-Right corner, Click on Type:Scale2D or Type:Scale3D

 

Then click on Filter. The result will show on the right side of the screen.
To click on Save to generate the whole series of the new images.

Scale Based Averaging

On Down-Right corner, Click to Type:SBAv2D or TypeSBAv3D

 

Then click on Filter. The result will show on the right side of the screen.
To click on Save to generate the whole series of the new images.
